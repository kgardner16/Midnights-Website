General Album Info:

This album was released on October 21, 2022. It was an instant success. Within less than 24 hours, it broke the record for the most streamed album on Spotify within a single day.

To see a full list of all the records broken by this album, click here!

To celebrate and promote the new album, Times Square showed a special ad featuring Taylor, and I got to go see it! (Pictured below)

The album also performed incredibly well on the Billboard Charts. In fact, Taylor became the first artist in history to simultaneously hold all top 10 spots on the Billboard Hot 100. Watch the video below to hear more about it!
